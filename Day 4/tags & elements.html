Subscript & Superscript

Subscript (<sub>): The subscript tag displays text slightly below the normal line. It is often used in chemical formulas. Example: H2O

Superscript (<sup>): The superscript tag displays text slightly above the normal line. It is often used in mathematical expressions. Example: 102 = 100
